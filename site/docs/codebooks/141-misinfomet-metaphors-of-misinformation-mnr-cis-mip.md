## Description

This project investigates how dominant metaphors for misinformation (e.g. “infodemic”, “information warfare”, “information pollution”) shape how the problem is conceptualised and which policy responses are prioritised at different system levels (micro, meso, macro). We conduct a Meta-Narrative Review (MNR) combined with Critical Interpretive Synthesis (CIS), complemented by a small MIP/MIPVU pilot on titles/abstracts and policy briefs. All protocols, extraction templates and pilot coding schemes will be shared openly on OSF once the preregistration is approved by the author team.
