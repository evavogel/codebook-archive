## Description

In the interdisciplinary research project "NOHATE – Overcoming crises in public communication about refugees, migration, foreigners", communication scholars, information scientists and computer linguists analyzed hate speech in user comments on refugees and immigration posted on German news websites and social media. On that basis, a machine learning model for automated hate speech detection was developed.
