## Description

We collected a broad set of cognitive measures related to the election, political leaders, and voters across five independent waves: (a) two weeks before the 2024 legislative election; (b) immediately after the 2024 election results became available; (c) seven weeks after the 2024 election; and (d) two weeks before the 2025 legislative election. A fifth wave was conducted with a sample of non-Portuguese, non-resident participants. The measures included assessments of perceived facial traits, candidate recognition, candidate evaluations, feeling thermometer ratings, political ideology, affective forecasting, and other related constructs.
